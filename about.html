What is BlackOrigin?

BlackOrigin is a technology company focused on building AI-powered platforms that empower creators, entrepreneurs, and communities globally.

What products has BlackOrigin built?

Our first products include Campus Music and OriginOS.

What is Campus Music?

Campus Music is a music streaming and discovery platform designed for student creators and listeners.

What is OriginOS?

OriginOS is an AI-powered personal life operating system designed to help individuals manage their time, finances, and goals intelligently.
