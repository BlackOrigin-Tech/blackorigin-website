Answers to common investor questions.

(Frequently asked)
What stage is BlackOrigin at?

BlackOrigin is an early-stage technology company founded in 2026 and based in Chicago, IL. Our first product, Campus Music, is live and active. Our second product, OriginOS, is currently in development.

Is BlackOrigin currently raising?

We are selectively engaging with investors who align with our long-term vision. If you are interested in learning more, submit an inquiry through the form above and we will follow up directly.

What is the business model?

BlackOrigin builds platform-native products with subscription and ecosystem-based revenue models. We focus on one product at a time — building depth before breadth — and reinvest into the next platform as each one matures.

What makes Campus Music defensible?

Campus Music is built natively for the student creator community — a segment that existing platforms treat as an afterthought. Its moat is network density within campuses, creator-first tooling, and a distribution channel that grows organically through school communities.

What is the long-term vision?

BlackOrigin's goal is to build an enduring technology ecosystem — not a feature factory. Each product we ship is designed to stand alone, scale independently, and compound in value over time. We are building for decades, not exit cycles.
