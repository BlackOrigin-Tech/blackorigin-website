The music platform built for campus culture.

Campus Music is a streaming and discovery platform built exclusively for the campus experience. Students can listen to curated playlists from their school community, surface emerging local artists, and follow the sounds that actually move their campus.

We built it because campus music culture is real, vibrant, and completely underserved by mainstream platforms. Campus Music gives it a home.
